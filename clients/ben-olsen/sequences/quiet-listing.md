Hi [first name],
Whether you stay fully private or eventually go public, the preparation work is the same — and it's where most of the outcome gets decided.
Before I introduce a home to my private buyer network, I do a forensic review: what's the realistic pricing story, what condition issues are worth addressing, what's missing from the presentation, and what needs to be disclosed upfront so buyers can move forward with confidence.
