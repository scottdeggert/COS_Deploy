Buy Before You Sell

If you're also selling a home, you don't have to wait for yours to close before you can move on the right property. Programs exist that remove the contingency entirely, so you can make a real offer on your next home without the timing pressure that kills most move-up deals.

Some listings in this area are run on a platform that shows offer activity in real time.
