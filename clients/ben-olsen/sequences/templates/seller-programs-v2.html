A few things worth knowing now.

How We Go to Market
  Superior presentation. Professional photography, floor plans, 3D immersive tours, and aerial Sky Tours on every listing. Not the luxury package. The baseline.
  Premium portal presence. Zillow Showcase and priority homes.com placement. Your home appears differently than most listings, and every inquiry routes to your listing team instead of a competing agent.
  The preparation is the negotiation. Before anything lists, I do a forensic review of the home: what needs addressing, what gets disclosed upfront, and what the right pricing story actually is. Listings structured this way attract stronger first offers and fewer surprises after the fact. The work happens before the sign goes up.

Programs Most Sellers Never Think to Ask About

Quiet Listing

I maintain a private, curated list of serious Bay Area buyers who've asked specifically to hear about Lamorinda properties before anything goes public. For the right seller, starting there means no MLS clock, no public foot traffic, and no neighborhood watching a days-on-market counter. If the right buyer is there, we find them cleanly. If not, a full market launch is the natural next step.

See How a Quiet Listing Works

BrightFlip

If your home needs work before it's ready to show, you don't have to choose between selling as-is or funding a renovation yourself. We have access to capital on attractive terms, and we manage the project. Nothing out of pocket until close. We run the numbers first and only recommend it when the math clearly favors you doing it.

Find Out If BrightFlip Makes Sense for Your Home

Buy Before You Sell

If you need to buy your next home before you can realistically sell this one, there are programs that let you make a non-contingent offer without waiting for your current home to close. The timing trap that forces most move-up sellers to compromise or rush doesn't have to apply to your situation.

See How Buy Before You Sell Works

Final Offer

On listings where I expect strong multi-offer competition, I use a platform that makes the offer process transparent. Every serious buyer sees activity in real time. Instead of one buyer guessing what it takes to win, the market finds the actual price. I decide whether it's right for each home. Not every listing needs it. When it does, it changes the outcome.
