A few things worth knowing.

How We Approach Senior Transitions
  Options before urgency. Sell, rent, stay put, or stage a gradual downsizing. The right path depends on what the move needs to accomplish — not what the market is doing this quarter. We start with your goals and work outward from there.
  Discretion when it matters. Many families navigating a senior transition value privacy — no public foot traffic, no neighborhood curiosity, no days-on-market clock while you're still deciding. How a home is positioned can respect that preference without sacrificing a strong outcome.
  Preparation reduces friction. Before anything moves forward, I review the home honestly: what condition issues are worth addressing, what gets disclosed upfront, and what the realistic pricing story is. When buyers come in fully informed, transactions tend to close cleaner — with fewer surprises for everyone involved.

Programs Worth Knowing About

Senior Planning

A planning resource for homeowners and families thinking through a later-life transition. Covers the questions that tend to come up first — staying versus moving, timing, equity, and what different paths actually look like in practice. Built for people who want to understand their options before anything needs to happen.

Explore the Senior Planning Resource

Quiet Listing

I maintain a private list of serious, pre-qualified buyers who've asked specifically to hear about Lamorinda properties before anything goes public. For sellers who value discretion — especially during a sensitive life transition — starting there means no MLS clock and no neighborhood watching a listing counter. If the right buyer is there, we find them cleanly. If not, a full market launch is always available.

See How a Quiet Listing Works

Buy Before You Sell

If you need to secure your next home before letting go of this one, there are programs that let you make a non-contingent offer without waiting for your current home to close. For seniors moving into assisted living, downsizing to a smaller place nearby, or relocating closer to family, that timing flexibility can remove the pressure that forces rushed decisions.

See How Buy Before You Sell Works
